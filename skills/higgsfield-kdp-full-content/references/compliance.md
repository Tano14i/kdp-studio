# KDP compliance checklist (Stage 5 gate)

Derived from Amazon KDP Content Guidelines, Metadata Guidelines, Guide to Kindle
Content Quality, and the AI Content policy. Reject and fix any violation before
the book moves to Stage 6. In case of conflict, the official Amazon policy wins
(kdp.amazon.com/help).

## Title / Subtitle
- Real book title only — no repeated generic keywords (journal, guide, book…).
- No placeholders (n/a, none, null, blank, unknown); no punctuation-only titles.
- No prices, no "free", no "bestseller", no "#1", no promo claims.
- No other authors', brands', or trademarked names.
- Title + subtitle combined ≤ 200 characters.

## Keywords (7 backend)
- Must accurately describe the book's real content.
- No misleading/unrelated terms; no competitor or other-author names; no brands.
- Banned: "free", "bestseller", "on sale", "new", "Kindle Unlimited", "%", prices.
- Prefer 2-3 word buyer-intent phrases; don't repeat title/subtitle words.

## Description
- Accurately represents the real content; no misleading claims.
- No fake reviews/testimonials or invented quotes.
- No external links / URLs; no requests to leave reviews.
- No references to bonus content or other books not inside this volume.

## Content
- Original and non-infringing: no plagiarism, copyright or trademark violation.
- Public-domain text only with substantial added value (annotations, new
  translation, illustrations, curated formatting). No public-domain spam.
- No offensive/illegal content (hate, sexual exploitation, pornography,
  terrorism apology).

## Quality
- No typos, broken formatting, low-quality images, or dead links.
- No duplicated/freely-available content without added value.
- Formatting must not confuse the reader or inflate page count artificially.
- Multi-story compilations: mark "Collection/Compilation/Series", sequential order.

## AI disclosure (required)
- AI-generated text/images/translations must be declared in the KDP publishing
  workflow, even after substantial edits. AI-assisted (brainstorming, grammar on
  your own text) does not require disclosure.
- Anti-spam: max 3 new titles per account per day.

## Consequences of violating
Book removal or content warning · withheld pending royalties on the removed
title · removed titles may stay flagged · repeated/egregious violations →
account suspension or termination.
